Editorial Policy

We aim for accuracy, transparency, and clear attribution.

Standards

  • • Verify claims with reliable sources; avoid misleading headlines.
  • • Separate news, opinion, and sponsored content with clear labels.
  • • Provide author byline and maintain author profile pages.

Attribution

When content is curated, we include a clear source reference or link.

Sponsored content

Sponsored posts must be labeled and must not imitate editorial news.
